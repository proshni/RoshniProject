Our goal

Project Roshni is a non profit organisation aimed at helping young children in India receive a decent education. We provide the students with free stationery, school bags and lunches which urges them to keep attending school. Our goal, is to teach the children enough English and arithmetic to be able to learn online themselves, or help them attend school. There are no fees for attending these schools, and the teachers are all volunteers offering us their time. Therefore to keep Roshni running, we need generous donations from you, our audience. Without your support, we wouldn't make it this far, and without your support, we won't be able to continue or provide free food, stationery and school bags to these children. Our aim is to open hundreds of schools in India, to educate young girls and boys in the rural region, where often many children are not able to attend school.

Read More

How we do it.

The process that Project Roshni follows is quite simple; we try to find individuals who are willing to offer some of their time to teach these children English and basic math, and make them able enough to learn other subjects of interest themselves through online websites or attend a proper school. Our goal is to teach these children basic knowledge that helps them in the future to choose whichever path they desire, whether it be attending a proper school or learning their favourite subjects themselves.
